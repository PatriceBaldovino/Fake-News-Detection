About This Website

Welcome to the Fake News Detection website! In today’s world, the rapid spread of misinformation poses a significant challenge. This website aims to address this issue by helping users analyze news articles and determine their authenticity.

What We Do

This platform allows you to input a news article and receive a prediction on whether it is likely to be real or fake. By leveraging advanced machine learning techniques, I aim to assist in identifying misinformation quickly and efficiently.

How It Works

  • The website uses a trained machine learning model, built on a dataset of verified and fake news articles.
  • When you submit an article, the model analyzes its content for patterns and features indicative of real or fake news.
  • Results are generated based on probabilities, offering you insights into the credibility of the article.

About the Project

This is a machine learning project designed to explore the capabilities of natural language processing (NLP) in identifying misinformation. The goal is to contribute to a more informed society by providing a tool to assist in critical evaluation of news content.
